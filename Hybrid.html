BMW M Hybrid Vehicles

These BMW M Vehicles combine electric motors with combustion force, to create more power while being efficient.

  • BMW i8
    • A Plug-in hybrid sports car that features a 1.5L 3-cylinder namely the B38K15T0 engine paired with an electric motor. Together this combination produce 369hp and 583Nm of torque.It has an electric range of up to 55km.
  • BMW XM
    • A flagship hybrid SUV housing the S68B44T0 engine which is a 4.4L V8, it has an electric motor. Its full output is 644hp and a 1000Nm of torque, it has an electric range of up to 88Km.
